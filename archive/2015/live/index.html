Prize Information & Submitting Your Hack

All prizing info is on our ChallengePost page.

To submit your hack, go to our ChallengePost page. Your team will have to sign up to add the hack to qualify for prizes. Challengepost makes it easier for us to keep track of hacks and (winning) hackers.

Don't forget, Challengepost will stop allowing hacks to be submitted at 10am Sunday morning. You can submit early and update your listing later.

Want a free Myo armband? Thalmic Labs is giving away 1 Myo armband and all HackYSU attendees are welcome to participate in the draw. Make sure you enter here: https://promosimple.com/ps/7108. This contest closes on April 20. Don't forget to try a Myo in the MLH hardware lab!

HackerRank Code Competition Saturday 6-9pm

Take a break from your projects to participate in a coding competition courtesy of HackerRank. You can participate at any time from 6 to 9pm Saturday night, and there are two difficulty levels: beginner and advanced.

Top participants win HackerRank t-shirts!

Here are the links to get started:
Beginner: https://www.hackerrank.com/hackysu-beg
Advanced: https://www.hackerrank.com/hackysu-adv

Find A Mentor

You can access the Mentor List to get in touch with a mentor, or just find one floating around in person - you'll notice mentors and event organizers by their black wristbands! They will either help you, or point you in the right direction. If you'd like to be included on the mentor sheet, contact an event organizer.
